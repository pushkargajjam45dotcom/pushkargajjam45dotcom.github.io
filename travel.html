How to Reach Solapur

[cite: 50]

By Road: Connected by highways to major cities like Pune (250 km), Hyderabad (300 km), Bengaluru (380 km), and Mumbai (400 km)[cite: 52, 55, 56, 57, 58]. Regular government and private buses operate[cite: 53].

By Rail: Solapur Railway Station is a major junction with trains connecting to Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, and more[cite: 60]. Rail travel is highly convenient[cite: 61].

By Air: Nearest major airports are Pune and Hyderabad[cite: 63, 64, 65].

Local Transport

[cite: 67]

Move around the city using auto rickshaws, taxis, and city buses[cite: 69, 70, 71]. Nearby pilgrimage towns are easily accessible by road[cite: 72].

How to Reach Solapur [cite: 50]

Solapur is well connected by highways to major cities like Pune (250 km), Hyderabad (300 km), and Mumbai (400 km)[cite: 52, 55, 56, 57].

By Rail: Solapur Railway Station is a major junction[cite: 60]. Rail travel is one of the most convenient ways to reach the city[cite: 61].

Local Transport [cite: 67]

Visitors can move around using auto rickshaws, taxis, and city buses[cite: 68, 69, 70, 71].

Festivals & Traditions

The culture reflects a mix of Marathi and Kannada traditions[cite: 40]. Festivals like the Siddheshwar Yatra and Ashadhi Wari see millions of devotees singing devotional songs[cite: 34, 37, 38].

Handloom and Textile Heritage [cite: 43]

Solapur is famous across India for its quality textiles, especially the Solapur Chaddars (bed sheets) and cotton towels[cite: 44, 45, 46].
